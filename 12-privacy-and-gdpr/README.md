# Privacy and GDPR

A practical guide to GDPR compliance for developers and founders. No law degree required.

---

## Why This Matters

GDPR fines can reach €20 million or 4% of global annual turnover — whichever is higher. But more immediately: users expect you to handle their data responsibly, and demonstrating that you do builds trust.

The good news: most of what GDPR requires is also good product practice.

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] Privacy policy exists and is accurate
- [ ] Users know what data is collected and why
- [ ] Lawful basis identified for each processing activity
- [ ] Cookie consent implemented correctly
- [ ] Users can request deletion of their data
- [ ] Breach notification process is in place
- [ ] Data is not retained longer than necessary
- [ ] Third-party processors have data processing agreements
