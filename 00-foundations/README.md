# Foundations: Security Principles for Developers

In 30 seconds: Security isn't an afterthought—it's a design choice. This section teaches you why breaches matter, how to think about threats before they happen, and why your mindset shapes what gets built. You'll learn the business case for security, the mental models that prevent attacks, and the processes that make security stick. Every developer on your team should read this.

## Why this matters

Security breaches cost companies millions in recovery, fines, and lost customers. But most attacks don't exploit exotic zero-days—they exploit missing basics: unencrypted data, missing input validation, weak authentication. This section gives you the framework to spot those gaps before users are affected.
