# Compliance Lite

A practical guide to the regulations that apply to SaaS founders — without the jargon.

---

## The Key Question

Compliance requirements depend on three things:
1. **Where your users are** (EU/UK → GDPR; California → CCPA; etc.)
2. **What data you process** (health data → HIPAA; payment cards → PCI DSS; etc.)
3. **What customers you serve** (enterprise B2B → SOC 2; US government → FedRAMP; etc.)

Start with [Which Compliance Applies to You](./which-compliance-applies-to-you.md) if you're not sure.
